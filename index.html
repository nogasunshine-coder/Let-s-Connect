Reading — Part One
Read slowly. Every word matters.
📋 הוראות — קריאה
  • קרא/י את הטקסט לאט — כל מילה חשובה
  • המילים המודגשות הן מאוצר המילים שלמדנו
  • חשוב/י: האם הטקסט מתאר את המצב שלך עכשיו?

1There's nothing in the world more important than family.

2Each family has its own rules, habits, and traditions.

3These things connect family members.

 

4However, today we are all busy working or studying long hours

5and we do not spend much quality time with our families.

6The result is that family connections are not as

7strong as they used to be in the past.

 

8What can we do about it?

🤔 Think about this as you read: The text says families are less connected today. But right now, because of the war and school closures, many of us are at home together. Is this different from what the text describes?
